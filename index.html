Good Questions

The things neighbors ask first.

Is battery power enough for
Florida grass?
Yes. Our equipment effortlessly powers through any type of grass, whether it's St. Augustine, Zoysia, or Bermuda, and the cut looks just as good, often better than gas.
What does lawn service cost?
Every lawn is a little different, so we do not post one flat price. Type your address for an instant estimate, and the in-person price always lands at or below it.
Do I need a contract?
No. Pricing stays the same year-round, we just visit more often in the summer growing season and less in winter, and you can cancel anytime with 24 hours notice.
What if it rains on my day?
Your visit is moved a day or two and confirmed by text. A visit is never simply lost.
Do I need to be home?
No. A gate code or a side-yard note in your booking covers it.
Is it safe for pets and kids right after?
Yes. Nothing is sprayed and nothing is burned,
so the lawn is ready to use the moment the gear is packed up.
